The Best Movies According to Sonya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Baby Boy

Baby Boy is considered a black classic. It is funny, motivating, and just entertaining.

Harlem Nights

Harlem Nights is once again a black classic. It is one of the best movies of all times. I have to say that it is funny, moving, entertaining, and just all around fantastic.

Willy Wonka

Now, this one is also a classic but for a very different movie. This is my all time favourite movie from when I was a kid. I love this movie!
